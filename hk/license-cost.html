‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a Hong Kong crypto license cost in 2026?

Total first-year cost ranges from HKD 8-25 million (USD 1-3.2M). This includes:

  • SFC application fee: HKD 780,000
  • Minimum capital: HKD 5M paid-up + HKD 3M liquid
  • Compliance setup: HKD 2-5M
  • Legal & advisory: HKD 1.5-3M
  • Technology: HKD 2-5M

What is the SFC license application fee?

The SFC charges HKD 780,000 for a new VATP license application. Annual renewal fee is HKD 295,000. These fees are non-refundable even if the application is rejected.

Can I reduce the cost?

Some ways to optimize costs:

  • White-label platform: Use existing trading technology instead of building from scratch
  • Outsource compliance: Use compliance-as-a-service for smaller operations
  • Shared office: Start in a co-working space before scaling
  • Phased approach: Start with minimum viable setup, expand after revenue

How long until I see ROI?

Most licensed exchanges take 18-36 months to break even, depending on:

  • Trading volume and fee structure
  • Market conditions
  • Customer acquisition efficiency
  • Operational efficiency
