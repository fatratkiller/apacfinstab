3. Operational Requirements

‚úÖ Compliance Checklist

  • Client Asset Segregation: 98% cold wallet, 2% hot wallet maximum
  • AML/CFT: Full compliance with FATF Travel Rule
  • KYC: Robust customer due diligence and ongoing monitoring
  • Custody: Licensed custodian or internal custody with SFC approval
  • Cybersecurity: ISO 27001 or equivalent standards
  • Token Admission: Due diligence process for listing new tokens
  • Investor Protection: Professional investors only (unless retail approved)
  • Market Surveillance: Systems to detect manipulation

4. Responsible Officers (ROs)

  • Minimum 2 ROs approved by SFC
  • Relevant experience in financial services (typically 5+ years)
  • Pass SFC fit and proper assessment
  • At least 1 RO with virtual asset experience
  • Executive Director must also be RO

‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a Hong Kong VASP license cost?

Direct fees:

  • Application fee: HKD 780,000
  • Annual renewal: HKD 295,000

Total setup costs: HKD 5-20 million typically, including legal, compliance, technology infrastructure, and operational setup.

How long does the licensing process take?

Typical timeline: 12-18 months from application submission, including:

  • Document preparation: 2-3 months
  • SFC initial review: 3-6 months
  • Q&A and clarifications: 3-6 months
  • On-site inspection and testing: 2-3 months
  • Final approval: 1-2 months

Can retail investors use licensed platforms?

Yes, but with restrictions. SFC-licensed platforms can serve retail investors if they:

  • Obtain specific retail authorization from SFC
  • Implement additional investor protection measures
  • List only tokens that pass retail admission criteria
  • Conduct suitability assessments

What tokens can be listed?

VATPs must conduct due diligence on each token. Generally:

  • Allowed: BTC, ETH, and major tokens meeting SFC criteria
  • Restricted: Security tokens (require additional Type 1 license)
  • Prohibited: Privacy coins, unregistered securities
