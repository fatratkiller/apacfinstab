‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How many licensed crypto exchanges are in Hong Kong?

As of February 2026, there are 10 fully SFC-licensed Virtual Asset Trading Platforms (VATPs) in Hong Kong, plus 11 platforms operating under deemed license (transitional arrangements). Only 3 platforms have retail investor access.

Is Binance licensed in Hong Kong?

No. Binance is not licensed by the SFC in Hong Kong and has not applied for a license. Hong Kong residents should only use SFC-licensed platforms for crypto trading.

Which platforms allow retail trading?

Only 3 platforms have been approved for retail investor access:

  • OSL Digital Securities
  • HashKey Exchange
  • PantherTrade

Other licensed platforms are limited to professional investors only (‚â•HKD 8 million portfolio).

What tokens can I trade on licensed platforms?

SFC-licensed platforms can only list tokens that pass their due diligence process. Generally available:

  • Bitcoin (BTC)
  • Ethereum (ETH)
  • Select major tokens approved by each platform

Privacy coins and unregistered security tokens are not permitted.
