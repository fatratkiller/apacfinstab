## What Happened?

On February 14, 2026, the Hong Kong Securities and Futures Commission (SFC) approved Victory Fintech to operate a digital asset trading platform. This is significant because:

1. **It's the first new license since June 2025** — the 8-month gap suggested a bottleneck
2. **It confirms the pathway works** — the licensing framework is functional
3. **It signals faster processing** — the SFC has added staff to handle applications

## The New Stablecoin Regime

Starting April 2026, the HKMA (not SFC) will begin issuing stablecoin issuer licenses. Key requirements:

- 100% reserve backing in high-quality liquid assets
- At-par redemption guarantee
- Reserves held with licensed custodians
- Hong Kong presence required
