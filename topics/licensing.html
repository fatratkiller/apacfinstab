Hong Kong SFC Approves First Solana Spot ETF

Hong Kong Securities and Futures Commission approves Solana spot ETF, expanding crypto ETF offerings beyond Bitcoin and Ethereum. Huaying Group AXG becomes the first brokerage firm in Hong Kong to issue virtual asset structured products under the SFC regulatory framework. This milestone signifies maturation of Hong Kongs institutional crypto derivatives market.

Hong Kong 2026 Budget Confirms Digital Asset Dealer and Custodian Licensing

Hong Kong 2026-27 Budget confirms legislation this year to license digital asset dealers and custodians, including OTC brokers and custody service providers. The budget also sweetens tax regime to attract crypto-friendly family offices as part of broader digital asset hub strategy.

Hong Kong to Award First Stablecoin Licenses in March, Expand to Dealers & Custodians

Hong Kong confirms first stablecoin licenses will be awarded in March 2026. The city is racing to compete with Singapore and Dubai for regional crypto infrastructure business.

Pakistan Senate committee approves draft Virtual Asset Act 2026, paving way for legal crypto trade. Pakistan Virtual Assets Regulatory Authority (PVARA) to grant licenses for crypto coin issuance and regulate crypto exchanges under new law. Follows PVARA regulatory sandbox launch earlier this month.

2026-02-24 US

Crypto.com Receives Conditional OCC National Trust Bank Charter

Crypto.com receives conditional approval from US Office of the Comptroller of the Currency (OCC) for a national trust bank charter. Upon full approval, Crypto.com National Trust Bank will operate as a qualified custodian for digital assets under direct OCC supervision. Significant milestone for institutional crypto custody in US market with implications for APAC operations.
