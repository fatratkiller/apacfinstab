## The Application Process: 6 Steps

### Step 1: Pre-Assessment (2-4 weeks)
Evaluate your business model against SFC requirements. Key questions:
- Are you providing a trading platform or just custody?
- Will you serve retail or professional investors only?
- What virtual assets will you list?

### Step 2: Corporate Setup (1-2 months)
- Register Hong Kong company
- Establish local office (physical presence required)
- Appoint minimum 2 Responsible Officers (ROs) with relevant experience

### Step 3: Build Compliance Infrastructure (3-6 months)
- AML/KYC systems (transaction monitoring, sanctions screening)
- Cybersecurity framework (penetration testing, incident response)
- Client asset segregation and custody procedures

### Step 4: Submit Application (1 month)
- Complete Form 1 and supporting documents
- Pay HKD 750,000 application fee
- Prepare for SFC questions

### Step 5: SFC Review (6-12 months)
- Multiple rounds of queries expected
- On-site inspections may occur
- External consultant reports often required

### Step 6: Approval & Launch (1-2 months)
- Final licensing conditions negotiated
- Public announcement
- Ongoing compliance obligations begin

## Common Misconceptions

❌ **Myth:** "You can operate from overseas with a Hong Kong license"  
✅ **Fact:** Local presence is mandatory. Key personnel including ROs must be based in Hong Kong.

❌ **Myth:** "The license covers all crypto activities"  
✅ **Fact:** The VASP license covers trading platforms only. Custody, OTC, and advisory may require separate licensing under 2026 proposals.

❌ **Myth:** "Deemed licensed operators can keep operating indefinitely"  
✅ **Fact:** Deemed licensing ended June 2024. Operators who didn't apply have been shut down.
