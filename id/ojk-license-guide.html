‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Who regulates crypto in Indonesia now?

As of January 10, 2025, OJK (Otoritas Jasa Keuangan / Financial Services Authority) regulates crypto in Indonesia, taking over from Bappebti.

Are existing Bappebti licenses still valid?

Yes. All licenses, approvals, and registrations issued by Bappebti remain valid as long as they don't conflict with current laws.

Can foreign exchanges serve Indonesian users?

Foreign exchanges targeting Indonesian users must comply with local regulations. Operating without proper licensing while actively marketing to Indonesians is prohibited.
