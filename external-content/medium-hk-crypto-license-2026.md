## The Two License Types

### VASP License (SFC)

For Virtual Asset Trading Platforms serving retail or professional investors.

| Requirement | Details |
|-------------|---------|
| **Regulator** | Securities and Futures Commission |
| **Fee** | HKD 750,000 |
| **Capital** | HKD 5M minimum |
| **Timeline** | 12-18 months |
| **Local Presence** | Required — office + key staff in HK |

### Stablecoin Issuer License (HKMA)

For issuing fiat-referenced stablecoins. New as of 2026.

| Requirement | Details |
|-------------|---------|
| **Regulator** | Hong Kong Monetary Authority |
| **Reserves** | 100% backing in HQLA |
| **Redemption** | At-par, within specified timeframe |
| **First Licenses** | Expected April 2026 |

## 6-Step Application Process

**Step 1: Pre-Application Assessment (3-6 months)**
Gap analysis against SFC requirements. Define your operating model, token listing policy, custody arrangements. Underestimating this stage is why most applicants fail.

**Step 2: Build Compliance Infrastructure (3-6 months)**
AML/KYC systems, transaction monitoring, travel rule compliance. Budget HKD 2-5M.

**Step 3: Appoint Responsible Officers**
Minimum two ROs with relevant experience, SFC approval required, must be HK-based. This is usually the biggest bottleneck.

**Step 4: Submit Application**
Online portal. HKD 750,000 fee. Business plan, compliance manual, IT security assessment, shareholder details.

**Step 5: SFC Review (12-18 months)**
Multiple Q&A rounds. Interviews with ROs. External cybersecurity assessments.

**Step 6: Approval-in-Principle → Full License**
Final setup requirements, insurance finalization, operational testing.

## 4 Misconceptions That Can Get You In Trouble

❌ **"We can operate now and apply later"**
→ Operating without a license is criminal. Transitional period ended June 2024. Up to 7 years prison + HKD 5M fine.

❌ **"Professional-only means lighter requirements"**
→ Still requires full VASP licensing. Only token listing restrictions differ.

❌ **"Our offshore entity can serve HK remotely"**
→ Actively marketing to HK residents triggers licensing regardless of incorporation.

❌ **"12-18 months is pessimistic"**
→ It's realistic. Plan accordingly.
