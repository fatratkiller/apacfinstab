What Changed in 2026?

Hong Kong's 2026-27 Budget, released in February 2026, confirmed major expansions to the virtual asset licensing framework. Here's what's new:

  • Stablecoin Issuer Licensing: The HKMA now administers a licensing regime for fiat-referenced stablecoin issuers. First batch of licenses expected in April 2026.
  • Expanded VASP Scope: Legislative proposals will extend licensing requirements to OTC trading desks, custody services, and potentially VA advisory services.
  • Accelerated Processing: The SFC has hired additional staff to reduce application backlogs. Victory Fintech received approval in February 2026 — the first new license since June 2025.

The Two License Types You Need to Know

1. VASP License (SFC)

Required for operating a Virtual Asset Trading Platform (VATP) that allows retail and/or professional investors to trade virtual assets. Administered by the Securities and Futures Commission (SFC).

Requirement Details
Regulator Securities and Futures Commission (SFC)
Application Fee HKD 750,000 (~USD 96,000)
Minimum Capital HKD 5 million paid-up capital
Timeline 12-18 months (typical)
Local Presence Required — office and key staff in HK
Insurance Professional indemnity + client asset protection

2. Stablecoin Issuer License (HKMA)

Required for issuing fiat-referenced stablecoins (FRS) in Hong Kong. Administered by the Hong Kong Monetary Authority (HKMA). This is new as of 2026.

Requirement Details
Regulator Hong Kong Monetary Authority (HKMA)
Reserve Requirements 100% backing in high-quality liquid assets
Redemption At-par redemption within specified timeframe
Custody Reserves held with licensed custodians
First Licenses Expected April 2026

Step-by-Step: Getting Your VASP License

1

Pre-Application Assessment (3-6 months)

Conduct a gap analysis against SFC requirements. Identify your operating model (retail vs. professional investors only), token listing policy, and custody arrangements. Many applicants fail because they underestimate this stage.

2

Build Compliance Infrastructure (3-6 months)

Implement AML/KYC systems compliant with Hong Kong's AMLO. Set up transaction monitoring, suspicious activity reporting, and travel rule compliance. Budget HKD 2-5M for technology and staffing.

3

Appoint Responsible Officers

You need at least two Responsible Officers (ROs) with relevant experience and SFC approval. They must be based in Hong Kong and pass fit-and-proper tests. Finding qualified ROs is often the biggest bottleneck.

4

Submit Application

File through the SFC's online portal. Application fee: HKD 750,000. Include business plan, compliance manual, IT security assessment, and details of all substantial shareholders.

5

SFC Review & Clarifications (12-18 months)

Expect multiple rounds of questions. The SFC will conduct interviews with ROs and may require external assessments of your cybersecurity and custody arrangements.

6

Approval-in-Principle → Full License

Once approved in principle, you'll need to complete final setup requirements before receiving your full license. This includes finalizing insurance arrangements and operational testing.

Common Misconceptions

❌ Myth: "We can operate now and apply later"
✅ Fact: Operating a VATP without a license is a criminal offense. The transitional period ended June 1, 2024. Unlicensed operators face up to 7 years imprisonment and HKD 5M fine.
❌ Myth: "We only serve professional investors, so requirements are lighter"
✅ Fact: Professional-investor-only platforms still require full VASP licensing. The main difference is in token listing restrictions, not compliance requirements.
❌ Myth: "Our offshore entity can serve HK customers remotely"
✅ Fact: Actively marketing to or serving Hong Kong residents triggers licensing requirements, regardless of where your entity is incorporated.
❌ Myth: "The 12-18 month timeline is pessimistic"
✅ Fact: Most successful applicants report 12-18 months. Some took longer. The SFC is thorough. Plan accordingly and don't promise stakeholders faster timelines.

Next Steps

If you're considering a Hong Kong crypto license:
