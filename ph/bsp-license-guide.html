üìù VASP License Requirements

Step 1: MSB Certificate First

Before applying for VASP registration, you must obtain a Certificate of Authority to operate as a Money Services Business (MSB) from the BSP.

VASP Requirements (BSP Circular 1108)

  • Philippine Corporation: Must be incorporated in the Philippines
  • Physical Presence: Office in the Philippines
  • Minimum Capital: As prescribed by BSP
  • AML/CFT Compliance: Full compliance with AMLA (RA 9160)
  • Cybersecurity: Robust security standards
  • Risk Management: Comprehensive risk framework
  • Consumer Protection: Clear disclosures, complaint mechanisms
  • Qualified Personnel: Experienced management team

‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Can I get a new crypto license in the Philippines?

Currently difficult. The BSP has maintained a moratorium on new VASP licenses since 2022, extended through 2025. Monitor BSP announcements for updates on when new applications will be accepted.

What's the difference between BSP and SEC regulation?

BSP regulates VASPs (exchanges, wallets, payment services). SEC regulates token offerings and CASPs. If you're running an exchange, you need BSP. If issuing tokens, you need SEC.

How much does a Philippines crypto license cost?

Specific costs depend on BSP requirements. You need to first obtain MSB certification, then VASP registration. Budget for significant capital requirements, legal costs, and compliance setup.
