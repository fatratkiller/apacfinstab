üìù License Requirements

Corporate Requirements

  • Thai registered company (limited company)
  • Minimum registered capital as per license type
  • Physical office in Thailand
  • Thai national directors (majority)
  • Qualified management with relevant experience

Operational Requirements

  • KYC/AML: Full compliance with AMLO requirements
  • Cybersecurity: Robust security systems and protocols
  • Auditor: SEC-approved auditor
  • Accounting: Proper accounting systems
  • Risk Management: Comprehensive risk framework
  • Customer Protection: Segregated client assets

‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a Thailand crypto license cost?

Application fee is THB 30,000. Annual license fee is THB 2.5 million. Minimum capital for exchanges is THB 50 million. Total first-year setup: THB 60-75 million (~USD 1.7-2.1M).

How long does approval take?

The SEC and MOF review process takes approximately 150 days. After approval, you must commence operations within 180 days.

Can foreign companies apply?

Yes, but you must establish a Thai company. Foreign ownership is permitted but majority Thai directorship is typically required.
