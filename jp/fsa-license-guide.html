üìù Registration Requirements

Corporate Structure

  • Japanese corporation (Kabushiki Kaisha - KK)
  • Head office in Japan
  • Japanese resident representative director
  • Minimum capital: ¬•10 million (higher recommended)

Operational Requirements

  • Cold Wallet Segregation: 95%+ of customer assets in cold storage
  • Asset Segregation: Customer assets separate from company assets
  • System Security: Multi-sig, penetration testing, 24/7 monitoring
  • AML/CFT: Full compliance with JAFIC guidelines
  • Travel Rule: Mandatory compliance

JVCEA Membership

  • Membership effectively mandatory
  • Additional self-regulatory rules
  • Token listing approval process
  • Annual audits and reporting
‚ö†Ô∏è Strict Token Listing: Japan has one of the strictest token listing processes. New tokens must be approved by JVCEA before trading, often taking 6+ months per token. Only ~30 tokens are widely approved.

‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a Japan crypto license cost?

FSA registration fee is only ¬•150,000 (~$1,000). However, total setup costs range from ¬•50-150 million (~$350K-$1M) including capital, systems, compliance, and JVCEA membership.

How long does registration take?

6-12 months typical. Japan FSA conducts thorough reviews, especially for security systems. Post-2018 hack reforms made the process more rigorous.

Can foreign companies apply?

Yes, but you must:

  • Establish a Japanese corporation (KK)
  • Have Japanese resident director
  • Physical presence in Japan
  • Japanese-speaking compliance team

What about crypto taxes in Japan?

Japan has high crypto taxes - gains are taxed as miscellaneous income at rates up to 55% (including local taxes). This is a significant consideration for traders.
