üìä ASIC & AFSL Requirements

When Do You Need an AFSL?

You need an AFSL if your crypto activities involve "financial products":

  • Derivatives: Crypto futures, options, CFDs
  • Managed Investments: Crypto funds, indices
  • Non-cash Payment Facilities: Some stablecoins
  • Securities: Tokenized securities
  • Volume threshold: >$10M annual or >$5K per customer (from 2026)

‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a license to run a crypto exchange in Australia?

Yes. All exchanges must register with AUSTRAC as a Digital Currency Exchange (DCE). From 2026, platforms exceeding $10M annual volume or $5K per customer also need an AFSL from ASIC.

How much does it cost?

DCE only: AUD 100-250K first year (registration free, but compliance costs)

DCE + AFSL: AUD 300K-800K first year

Australia is relatively affordable compared to Hong Kong or Singapore for larger operations.

How long does registration take?

AUSTRAC DCE: 1-4 weeks (straightforward online process)

ASIC AFSL: 3-6 months (more complex, requires documentation)

What about crypto taxes?

Australia taxes crypto as Capital Gains Tax (CGT). Rates up to 45% for short-term gains, with 50% discount for assets held >12 months. This is higher than Singapore/Hong Kong (0%).
