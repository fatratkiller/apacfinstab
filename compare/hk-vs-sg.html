Which is cheaper: Hong Kong or Singapore crypto license?

Singapore is significantly cheaper. MAS license fee is SGD 1,500 vs Hong Kong SFC fee of HKD 780,000. Total first-year costs: Singapore SGD 1-2M (~USD 750K-1.5M) vs Hong Kong HKD 8-25M (~USD 1-3.2M).

Can I get both licenses?

Yes, many large exchanges hold licenses in both jurisdictions. This gives maximum market coverage and regulatory credibility. Budget USD 2-5M for dual licensing in the first year.

Which has better banking access?

Singapore. Banks like DBS, OCBC, and UOB are more crypto-friendly. Hong Kong banking for crypto firms has historically been challenging, though improving.
