â“ FAQs

Is Singapore's MPI license recognized in Australia?

No. Each jurisdiction requires separate licensing. There's no mutual recognition agreement for crypto licenses. However, your Singapore track record can help demonstrate compliance capability in Australia applications.

What about Australia's upcoming Digital Assets framework?

Australia's Treasury is developing a dedicated digital assets licensing framework. Expected to roll out 2026-2027. This may simplify requirements but is not yet finalized. Current applicants should plan for AFSL pathway.

Which has stricter AML requirements?

Both are FATF-compliant with strong AML/CFT requirements. Singapore's Travel Rule is already active (since Jan 2025). Australia's AUSTRAC requirements are robust but Travel Rule implementation is still pending.

Can I serve Australian customers from Singapore?

Technically possible but risky. ASIC has extraterritorial reach for services targeting Australians. Many Singapore exchanges block Australian IPs without local licenses. Safe approach: get Australian license for Australian customers.
