üìù License Requirements

Corporate Requirements

  • Singapore-incorporated company (Pte Ltd)
  • Permanent place of business in Singapore
  • At least 1 Executive Director resident in Singapore
  • At least 1 Director who is Singapore citizen or PR

Capital Requirements (MPI)

  • Base Capital: SGD 250,000 minimum
  • Security Deposit: SGD 100,000 or 30% of avg daily e-money float
  • Safeguarding: Customer funds in trust account or bank guarantee

Compliance Requirements

  • AML/CFT: Full compliance with MAS Notice PSN02
  • Travel Rule: Mandatory from March 2026
  • Risk Management: Enterprise risk framework required
  • Technology Risk: MAS Technology Risk Management Guidelines
  • Cybersecurity: Incident reporting within 1 hour

üìÖ Application Process

Step 1 Pre-Application (1-2 months)

Incorporate Singapore entity, prepare business plan, compliance framework, AML policies

Step 2 Submit Application

Apply via MAS portal with all required documents, pay SGD 1,500 fee

Step 3 MAS Review (3-6 months)

MAS reviews documents, compliance protocols, risk management, may request clarifications

Step 4 In-Principle Approval

If satisfied, MAS grants IPA with conditions to fulfill before final license

Step 5 Fulfill Conditions (2-4 months)

Complete systems setup, hire compliance team, establish bank relationships

Step 6 License Granted

Full license issued, can commence DPT services

Total Timeline: 6-12 months typical

‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a Singapore crypto license cost?

MAS license fees are minimal: SGD 1,000 (SPI) or SGD 1,500 (MPI). However, total first-year costs including compliance, legal, and operations range from SGD 500,000 to SGD 2 million.

How long does it take to get approved?

Typical timeline is 6-12 months. As of February 2026, MAS has streamlined the process for institutional applicants. Well-prepared applications can be faster.

Can foreign companies apply?

Yes, but you must:

  • Incorporate a Singapore subsidiary (Pte Ltd)
  • Have at least 1 resident director
  • Establish physical presence in Singapore
  • Appoint local compliance officer

What's the Travel Rule requirement?

Singapore mandates Travel Rule compliance from March 2026. DPT service providers must collect and transmit originator/beneficiary information for transfers above SGD 1,500.
